About Zoomer Africa

Zoomer Africa is a revolutionary social media platform developed by Transparent Hub Uganda Limited (THUL). As the leading platform for authentic African storytelling and community engagement, our mission is to amplify African voices and create meaningful connections across the continent.

Our Platform

Connecting African voices across the continent with cutting-edge technology

  • Developed by Transparent Hub Uganda Limited (THUL)
  • Focus on authentic African storytelling
  • Digital inclusion and empowerment initiatives
  • Cross-cultural understanding and dialogue
  • Professional networking platform for Africa
